Who are Manchester United?

Manchester United Football Club is a professional football club based in Old Trafford, Greater Manchester, England. Regarded as historically the best football team from England, they compete in the Premier League, the top flight of English football.

Manchester United have won the joint-record number of trophies in English club football, including a record 20 League titles, 12 FA Cups, five League Cups and a record 21 FA Community Shields. They have won the European Cup/UEFA Champions League three times, and the UEFA Europa League, the UEFA Cup Winners' Cup, the UEFA Super Cup, the Intercontinental Cup and the FIFA Club World Cup once each. In 1968, under the management of Matt Busby, they became the first English club to win the European Cup.
